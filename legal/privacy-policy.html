Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

IamSafe ("we," "us," or "our") operates the IamSafe mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and optionally your phone number. We also store your selected role (senior or caregiver) and timezone.

Check-in data: We record the date and time of each daily check-in you submit.

Selfie photos: If you enable the selfie feature, photos you take during check-in are stored securely. Selfies are automatically deleted after 30 days.

Emergency contacts: Names, email addresses, and optionally phone numbers of contacts you designate to receive alerts.

Device tokens: We collect Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens to send push notification reminders. These tokens are device identifiers, not personal information.

Subscription information: Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat and the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We receive entitlement status (e.g., "premium") but do not have access to your payment details.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts from your phone's address book
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Behavioral or biometric metadata
  • Health or medical data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the daily check-in service and alert your designated contacts if a check-in is missed
  • To send push notification reminders before your check-in window closes
  • To display check-in history and selfie photos to linked caregivers
  • To manage your subscription and premium features

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as follows:

  • Emergency contacts: When you miss a check-in, we send an alert (email or SMS) to contacts you have designated. Alerts include your name and the fact that a check-in was missed. Selfie photos are shared only with linked caregivers who have the App installed.
  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and file storage; Resend for email delivery; and Twilio for SMS delivery. These providers process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies.
  • Subscription management: RevenueCat processes subscription events. Apple and Google process payments.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase services located in the us-central1 region (United States). Selfie photos are stored in private buckets and accessed only via time-limited signed URLs (1-hour expiry).

Authentication tokens are stored on your device using encrypted secure storage, never in plain-text preferences.

6. Data Retention

  • Check-in records: Retained for 90 days (free tier) or indefinitely (premium). Records older than 90 days are permanently deleted for free-tier users.
  • Selfie photos: Automatically deleted after 30 days for all users.
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account, which permanently removes all associated data including check-ins, selfies, contacts, and settings.
  • Opt-out of alerts: Emergency contacts can unsubscribe from email alerts or reply STOP to opt out of SMS alerts at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

IamSafe is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
