1. Description of Service

IamSafe is a daily safety check-in app designed for senior citizens living alone. Users ("Seniors") tap a button daily to confirm they are safe. If a Senior misses their check-in window, designated contacts ("Emergency Contacts") are automatically notified via email or SMS.

Caregivers can link to a Senior's account to view check-in history and status through the App.

2. Important Limitations

IamSafe is not an emergency service. The App does not replace 911, medical alert systems, or professional medical care. We do not monitor health conditions, detect falls, or provide real-time location tracking.

Alert delivery depends on internet connectivity, email/SMS provider availability, and the Senior's device being powered on. We cannot guarantee alerts will be delivered within a specific timeframe.

Do not rely on IamSafe as your sole safety measure. It is designed as a supplementary daily check-in tool.
